Care & Maintenance

Proper care and maintenance of your marble products will ensure their longevity and beauty. Follow these guidelines to keep your marble in pristine condition.

Cleaning

Use a pH-neutral cleaner or a mild soap with water to clean your marble surfaces. Avoid using acidic or abrasive cleaners.

Sealing

Seal your marble periodically to protect it from staining and etching. The frequency of sealing depends on the type of marble and the amount of use it gets.

Protection

Use coasters, trivets, and placemats to protect your marble surfaces from heat and spills. Avoid placing heavy objects on your marble to prevent cracking and chipping.

Repair

If your marble gets scratched or chipped, contact a professional stone care specialist to repair the damage.
