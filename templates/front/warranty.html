Warranty

Ardi Marble offers a comprehensive warranty on all of our products. Please read below for more details.

Warranty Period

All products come with a warranty period of one year from the date of purchase.

What is Covered

This warranty covers any defects in material or workmanship under normal use during the warranty period.

What is Not Covered

This warranty does not cover any problem that is caused by conditions, malfunctions, or damage not resulting from defects in material or workmanship.

How to Claim

To obtain warranty service, you must first contact us to determine the problem and the most appropriate solution for you.
